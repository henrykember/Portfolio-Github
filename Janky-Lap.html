A thing I did way back when, exploring the levels of repetitive rhythm in the act of lane swimming. Moving the first rope triggers a sound. Move the second, trigger video. Move the third, modulate the effects on both. (NB this was done before I had any clue how to document things. And it was filmed on a day when the rickety software I wrote decided to crash. sAs such, it's pretty unprepossessing. But it's the though that counts...?)
