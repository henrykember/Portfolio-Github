Art Piece
My girlfriend Julia and I were asked to participate in 'Housework' - a series of performance pieces done by Redfern artists in their own homes. We locked ourselves in our room and communicated with visitors only through Skype. By limiting our responses, and remaining completely silent, visitors were unsure if we were actually there or not. This tension led to some confused reactions, and a watermelon being stolen from our fridge. (The Housework documentary below is courtesy of curator/artist Di Smith.)
